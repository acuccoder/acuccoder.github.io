1. Asteroids

About:
Asteroids is a game that is like the old asteroids game from 1979, but with 3 types of asteroids, no flying saucers and better graphics. It might take a while to load because it is a big project.

Controls:
To control the spaceship, use WASD or arrow keys to move around, and use the space bar to shoot a laser. To aim, move the mouse.


2. Platformer

About:
This is a platformer with enemies, spikes, doors, keys, coins, and most importantly, a player. When you get to the "You Win" level, jump to the text to get to the win screen.

Controls:
To play, use WASD or the arrow keys. Jump on enemies, but don't let them touch you. The doors can squish you if you stand underneath them for too long.

How to wall jump:
Press up and left/right at the same time and hold left/right until you get to a wall. Then, you will start sliding. Please note that you can't continue without a wall to the left and the right of the player. Press up again while you are sliding to jump to the other wall. Keep doing this until you reach where you want to go.
